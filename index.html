About Me

Front-End Developer!

Hello, my name is Nicolás Bianchi, I am 24 years old and I am from Buenos Aires, Argentina.

I've been close to a computer since an early age, and been passionate about it ever since.

In the year 2021, together with the Covid-19 pandemic, I began to investigate the world of programming since I liked it since I was a child. And I began to study it in a self-taught way with free internet bootcamps. Starting in 2022 I decided to pay for a bootcamp to be able to start the Full-Stack Programmer career since I liked the idea of having challenges on "real" projects that they asked me for in the bootcamp and obtaining the corresponding certificates for each course that I managed to pass. Currently I am already received as a front-end developer with three courses (web development, javascript and react.js). I am currently in the back-end development course and I love it!

I am looking for my first job as a front-end developer to be able to provide all my knowledge and help to the company and start my professional career so that I can learn even more about this beautiful profession and passion that is programming.

I have work experience in Microsoft Visual Basic (Excel) and also in fiber optics, administration and logistics.

I have minimal knowledge of English and Portuguese languages.
